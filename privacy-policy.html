1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how the Kaset mobile application ("Kaset", "the Application", "we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Application. Kaset is a personal media tracker for movies, television shows, and anime, available for iOS and Android. This Policy applies to information processed through the Application. Please read it carefully. By downloading, accessing, or using Kaset, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy. If you do not agree with it, please do not use the Application.

2. Information We Collect

Kaset is designed to function without requiring you to provide personal information to us. We do not operate accounts and we do not maintain servers that receive or store your content. Information you create in the Application is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us. Such information includes:

CategoryExamplesStorage Location
Library and Tracking DataMovies, shows, and anime you add; watch status and progress; watch history and datesOn your device only
Personal ContentRatings, reviews, notes, and tags you createOn your device only
Organizational DataLists and collections you createOn your device only
Cached Catalog DataTitles, artwork, descriptions, and details previously retrieved for faster reuse and offline viewingOn your device only
Application PreferencesSelected language and interface settingsOn your device only

This information is stored in a private database on your device and remains under your control. We do not receive a copy of it. It may be included in your device's own backups only if you have enabled operating-system backups (for example, iCloud Backup or Google backup), which are governed by Apple's or Google's respective privacy policies rather than this Policy.

When the Application communicates with the third-party services described in Section 6, those services necessarily receive limited technical information required to fulfill the request, such as your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address and standard request headers. This is inherent to how internet communication works and is not used by us to identify or track you.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

Kaset is built to know as little about you as possible. Specifically, the Application does not:

  • require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address, name, telephone number, or password;
  • collect or store your real-world identity, contacts, photographs, calendar, precise or approximate location, or health information;
  • use advertising identifiers (such as Apple's Identifier for Advertisers or the Android Advertising ID), and it does not request App Tracking Transparency permission;
  • include third-party analytics, advertising, marketing, or social-media tracking software development kits (SDKs); or
  • maintain servers that receive, store, or process a copy of your library or personal content.

4. How We Use Information

Because your content is stored locally, it is used solely to provide the Application's features on your device — for example, to display your library, calculate your on-device viewing statistics, and organize your lists. The limited technical information described in Section 2 is used only to complete the requests you initiate (retrieving title information and downloading updates). We do not use any information to build a profile of you, to advertise to you, or to make automated decisions about you.

5. How Information Is Shared and Disclosed

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes, and we do not "sell" or "share" personal information as those terms are defined under applicable privacy laws, including the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and United States state privacy laws. Because your content remains on your device, there is nothing for us to sell or disclose. The only third parties that receive any information are the service providers described in Sections 6 and 9, and only the limited technical information inherent to making those requests. We may disclose information only where we are legally required to do so; in practice, however, we hold no personal information that could be disclosed.

6. Third-Party Services

To display information, artwork, and search results for movies, television shows, and anime, the Application retrieves data from a public media catalog service:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — privacy policy.

Requests to this service contain only what is necessary to locate and display a title — for example, the search text you enter, a title identifier, or your selected language so that results are returned in that language. They do not include your identity, your library, your notes, or any personal profile, because the Application maintains none to send. All such requests are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not control the independent data practices of this provider; please review its privacy policy, linked above. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

The Application also relies on a software update delivery service to provide the updates described in Section 9. Links to third-party sites and services are provided for convenience, and we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

7. Importing Your Data

Kaset allows you to import your existing watch history from other services, such as TV Time and Sofa. You begin an import by selecting an export file that you already have on your device. The file you select is read and processed entirely on your device. Its contents are not uploaded to us or to any third party. The imported records are stored locally alongside the rest of your library and are treated in the same manner as data you enter yourself. The Application accesses only the specific file you select; it does not scan or read other files on your device.

8. Permissions

The Application requests only the minimum access necessary, and only at the time it is needed:

  • File and Document Access — used solely so that you can select an import file, as described in Section 7. The system file picker allows you to choose a single file, and the Application receives only that file.
  • Network Access — used to retrieve title information from the catalog service, as described in Section 6, and to download application updates, as described in Section 9.

The Application does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, photographs, calendar, reminders, or health data.

9. Software Updates

Kaset uses over-the-air updates so that we can deliver fixes and improvements without always requiring a full application-store release. When the Application checks for and downloads an update, standard technical request information — such as your device's IP address, platform, and the Application's current version — is processed by the update delivery service so that it can serve the correct files. This information is used only to deliver software updates and is not used to build a profile of you or to track your activity.

10. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The Application contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, no third-party trackers, and no crash- or usage-reporting that transmits your data off the device. We do not build advertising or behavioral profiles, we do not use cookies or similar cross-application identifiers, and we do not track you across other applications or websites. Any statistics shown within the Application (for example, viewing counts or "insights") are calculated on your device from your own local data and are never transmitted.

11. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you keep the Application installed and choose to keep the data. You remain in full control:

  • You may permanently erase everything you have tracked at any time from Settings → Delete All Data within the Application. This removes your library, watch history, notes, ratings, tags, and collections.
  • Uninstalling the Application removes its local database and preferences from your device.

Because we hold no copy of your data on any server, these actions are complete and irreversible, and there is nothing further for us to delete on your behalf.

12. Data Security

Your data is stored within the Application's private, sandboxed storage on your device, which is isolated from other applications by the operating system and protected by your device's own security features (such as your passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, or Android biometric lock). All network requests the Application makes are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS (TLS) connections. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure; however, keeping your data on your device and avoiding accounts and servers meaningfully reduces the ways in which it could be exposed.

13. Children's Privacy

Kaset is a general-audience application and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction, such as 16 in parts of the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the Application requires no account and collects no personal information, no such information is gathered from users of any age. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us; as noted, however, the Application is designed so that no personal information is transmitted to us.

14. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you reside, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict the processing of your information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. Kaset is designed to honor these principles by its very architecture:

  • Access and Portability — your data resides on your device, where you may view it directly at any time.
  • Correction — you may edit or update any entry within the Application.
  • Deletion — you may erase all data from within the Application or by uninstalling it, as described in Section 11.
  • Objection and Restriction — the Application performs no profiling, automated decision-making, or targeted advertising to which you might object.

For residents of the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom: to the limited extent that the technical information described in Sections 2 and 9 constitutes personal data, the lawful basis for processing it is our legitimate interest in providing the core functionality you have requested (displaying title information and delivering updates). For residents of California and other United States states with applicable privacy laws: we do not sell or share personal information, and we do not process sensitive personal information for any purpose that would require an opt-out. For residents of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia: to the extent the Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) applies, you retain your rights to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data; because your data remains on your device and we hold no copy on any server, the Application is designed to honor these rights directly. Because we do not maintain your data on our servers, most requests can be satisfied by you directly within the Application. If you have questions or wish to exercise a right, please contact us using the details in Section 17.

15. International Users and Data Transfers

Kaset may be used worldwide. Your personal data remains on your device in your country. The limited technical requests to the service providers described in Sections 6 and 9 may be processed on servers located in other countries, including the United States, and operated by those independent providers under their own privacy policies. By using the Application, you understand that these standard network requests may be handled internationally.
